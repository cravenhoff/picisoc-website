We promote an open, inclusive and collaborative internet for everyone in the Pacific Islands

Together with our members and partners, we work towards ensuring an equitable, open and accessible internet for everyone in the Pacific Islands.

The Internet Society - Pacific Islands Chapter advocates for affordable, fair and secure internet access for Pacific Islanders.

Pacific-First Internet Representation

We represent the interests of the Pacific Islands Community, in line with the work of the Internet Society.

Parnternships for a Better Internet

We focus on local issues and developments and act as an impartial advisor to governments and the public on matters of significant interest to Pacific Island people concerning the Internet and ICT in general.

Promote Internet-First Initiatives

We promote initiatives that enable an equitable, safe, open and inclusive internet for all in the Pacific Islands.
